Charles Scrase

Strategic Advisor

Charles is an experienced business leader, with 15 years of strategy, sales, operations and marketing experience at Google, where he built sales and operations organizations from the ground up. He has significant experience building and leading teams, having managed global, regional and national teams across dozens of countries and offices, as well as having led the 500 person Google Ann Arbor office for more than five years.

Charles particularly enjoys helping teams define their vision and strategy, and then build the required business systems and processes to execute effectively. He has helped a variety of organizations at Google and beyond achieve a high level of performance by focusing on developing strategy, building strong organization culture, defining clear operational principles and helping teams hold themselves accountable.

Charles also has a deep understanding of customer acquisition and marketing strategy, having worked across a variety of customer facing functions during a period of dramatic transformation in how businesses and organizations of all types communicate with and generate business from existing and new customers.

In addition to his experience at Google, Charles also has experience as an entrepreneur in both business and social domains. He is a former Peace Corps volunteer who founded his own educational non-profit (which is still thriving today), his own real estate investment business and his own strategic advisory firm. He has an B.A. in English from the University of Michigan and an MBA with a focus on Leadership & Ethics from Duke University.

Charles is based in Ann Arbor, Michigan, where he and his wife Roxie are raising their family of three kids ages 9, 12 and 15.

Helped to define the new structure, define new roles, and then helped to manage the implementation of the reorganization, including developing the change management communications.
